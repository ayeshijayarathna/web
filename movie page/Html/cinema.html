Cinema Multiplex-C2

Accessible from the new Cinema Multiplex entrance, on Columbo, Linal Mawatha, and by a Level 3 direct connection to the mall, Cinema 2 is situated on Level 3. Superior 2K laser projection with 3D capability and a large Dolby 7.1 surround sound system are highlights of the C2. C2 makes effective use of available space without sacrificing the greatest moviegoing experience. With slightly softer seats than C1, it can accommodate 70 spectators with uninterrupted views all around. For your next party, corporate gathering, or private screening, C2 is the ideal location; find out more here.

Cinema Lobby

A place that is both breathtaking and simple, and that appeals to the movie buff in you. Before you even walk inside the theater, take in the sights and sounds of the film.
