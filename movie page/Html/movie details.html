WICKED

Wicked is an upcoming movie adaptation of the hit Broadway musical of the same name, which itself is based on Gregory Maguire's novel Wicked: The Life and Times of the Wicked Witch of the West. The story reimagines the world of The Wizard of Oz from the perspective of Elphaba, the misunderstood "Wicked Witch of the West," and her unlikely friendship with Glinda, the "Good Witch." It explores themes of friendship, identity, and societal prejudice, providing a fresh take on the classic tale. The film features enchanting music, dazzling visuals, and a compelling narrative that dives into the backstories of beloved characters from Oz.
